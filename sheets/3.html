Toad in the Hole (Vegetarian Version)

Hop into a world of whimsy with this Toad in the Hole! But wait—no toads here, just fluffy Yorkshire pudding cradling delicious veggie sausages like they’re on a cozy cloud. It’s like a warm hug from Grandma but with a cheeky twist! Drizzle some gravy over it, and you’ve got yourself a dish that would make even the grumpiest of goblins smile. It’s hearty, it’s silly, and it’s perfect for anyone who loves a good laugh while they eat!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk (or plant-based milk)
  • 2 large eggs (or flaxseed eggs for vegan)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetarian sausages

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk together flour, milk, eggs, salt, and pepper until smooth.
  3. Place vegetarian sausages in a baking dish and pour batter over them.
  4. Bake for about 25-30 minutes until the batter is puffed and golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with gravy or your favorite sauce.
