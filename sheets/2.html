Indonesian Fried Garlic Peanuts (Kacang Bawang)

Crunch, crunch, crunch! These little nuggets of joy are the ultimate snack attack! Indonesian Fried Garlic Peanuts are not just peanuts; they’re flavor bombs coated in crispy garlic goodness. Each handful is like a party in your mouth where garlic is the DJ and peanuts are the dance floor! Perfect for munching while watching your favorite shows or sharing with friends—though you might want to keep them all to yourself because they’re just that addictive!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups raw peanuts
  • 5 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • Oil for frying
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the sliced garlic and fry until golden brown, then remove and set aside.
  3. In the same oil, add the raw peanuts and fry until they are golden and crunchy.
  4. Drain the peanuts on paper towels and season with salt while still warm.
  5. Mix in the fried garlic before serving.
