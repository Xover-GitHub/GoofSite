Portobello Mushroom Burgers with Crispy Onions and Miso Mayonnaise

Imagine a burger so big and juicy that it could make a T-Rex jealous! This towering delight features meaty Portobello mushrooms that are grilled to perfection, topped with crispy onions that crunch like a thousand tiny fireworks in your mouth. And let’s not forget the miso mayonnaise—it’s like a creamy hug for your taste buds, adding a savory twist that dances on your tongue! This burger is not just food; it’s an experience that makes you want to shout “YAY!” after every bite.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large portobello mushrooms
  • 1 cup crispy fried onions
  • 1/2 cup miso mayonnaise (mix mayonnaise with miso paste)
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce and tomato for toppings

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill or skillet over medium heat.
  2. Clean the portobello mushrooms and remove the stems.
  3. Grill the mushrooms for about 5 minutes on each side until tender.
  4. Toast the burger buns on the grill for a minute.
  5. Assemble the burgers: spread miso mayonnaise on the bun, add grilled mushrooms, top with crispy onions, lettuce, and tomato.
  6. Serve immediately.
